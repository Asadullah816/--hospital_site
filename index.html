What is Endoscopic Spine Surgery?

Endoscopic spine surgery is a minimally invasive surgical technique used to treat various spinal conditions and disorders. A thin, flexible endoscope is placed into the damaged area through a series of tiny incisions. A camera and light source are built inside the endoscope, giving the surgeon a good image of the spinal structures on a display...
